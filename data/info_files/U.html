Because the United States file is so long, special headers are provided to indicate major file divisions.

A relatively small group of United States entries without subdivision (i.e., headings for the United States as a corporate or geographic entity) are at the beginning of the file.

United States entries with corporate or subject subdivisions are grouped into 10 major subfiles. The subfiles for Congress and History contain all subdivisions beginning with those specific terms. Others contain a range of subdivisions, as indicated. To find United States headings with a specific subheading, select the appropriate range. The first level of subheadings are arranged alphabetically, with further subarrangement as indicated.
