Entries for individual editions of the complete New Testament, and works about those specific editions, are arranged by language and then by publication date. Subject entries for works about New Testaments that have appeared in each language follow entries for editions. These subject entries are interfiled with the subdivisions Paraphrases and Selections. Subject entries for the complete New Testament follow all such entries for editions.

EXAMPLE:

Bible. N.T. Apache. 1966
Bible. N.T. English. 1850
Bible. N.T. English. 1934
BIBLE. N.T. ENGLISH--HISTORY
Bible. N.T. English. Selections. 1912
BIBLE. N.T. ENGLISH--VERSIONS
Bible. N.T. Greek. 1823
BIBLE. N.T.--ADDRESSES, ESSAYS, LECTURES
BIBLE. N.T.--BIOGRAPHY
