General Information

Books and groups of books are entered under New Testament or Old Testament, e.g., Bible. N.T. Revelation, or Bible. O.T. Pentateuch. Entries under each Testament are arranged alphabetically. Bible sub-headings that do not include a designation of either Testament are references.

EXAMPLE:

Bible. N.T. Corinthians
Bible. N.T. Gospels
Bible. N.T. Mark
Bible. O.T. Genesis
Bible. O.T. Minor prophets
Bible. O.T. Ruth
Bible. Ruth SEE Bible. O.T. Ruth

Paraphrases

Cataloging treatment of Bible paraphrases changed in 1968.

Paraphrases of Bible parts cataloged through 1967 were entered under Bible. [Part].[Language]. Paraphrases, e.g., Bible. N.T. Gospels. Latin. Paraphrases. Similar works cataloged after that date are entered under the subject heading BIBLE. [PART]--Paraphrases, [LANGUAGE], e.g., BIBLE. O.T. GENESIS--PARAPHRASES, FRENCH.
