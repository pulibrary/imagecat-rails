Included under this uniform title are collected editions of the author's works in this type. Arrangement within the file is:
  1. Complete editions in the original language, arranged by publication date.
  2. Selections in the original language, arranged by publication date.
  3. Complete editions in translation, arranged alphabetically by language, subarranged by publication date.
  4. Selections in translation, arranged by publication date, following the complete editions in each language.

For editions of an individual work in this form, see under its title following the Individual Titles card.
