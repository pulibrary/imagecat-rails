Entries for individual editions of the complete Old Testament, and works about those specific editions, are arranged by language and then by publication date. Subject entries for works about Old Testaments that have appeared in each language follow entries for editions. These subject entries are interfiled with the subdivisions Paraphrases and Selections. Subject entries for the complete Old Testament follow all such entries for editions.

EXAMPLE:

Bible. O.T. Dutch. 1666
Bible. O.T. English. 1907
Bible. O.T. English. 1953
Bible. O.T. Hebrew. 1893
