FORENAMES

Entries under this name are arranged as follows:

  1. Saints, by the first word of the designation
  2. Popes, numerically
  3. Sovereigns, alphabetically by country, then by number
  4. Princes and nobility, alphabetically by country, then numerically
  5. Other designations, in alphabetical order of the appellative
  6. Compound forenames follow single forenames of each category but precede single forenames in the next category.
Compound forenames area arranged alphabetically by the second part of the name.
