Here are entered the individual works which make up the Appendix Vergiliana, arranged in the following groupings and order: Aetna, Catalepton, Ciris, Copa, Culex, Dirae (Lydia), Priapae. Within each title grouping, works are arranged as follows: Latin versions, by date of publication; Translations, arranged alphabetically by language of translation, and subrarranged by date within each language group; the particular work as a subject.
